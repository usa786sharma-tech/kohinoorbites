Kohinoor Bites Pizza & More blends desi flavor with crowd-favorite comfort food. The vibe is bold, modern and premium — dark interiors, warm lights, neon glow, and menu items that feel familiar but hit differently.

That means crisp pizzas loaded with butter chicken, achari gobi, paneer, pesto and curry flavors, plus chaat, wings, pasta, shakes, mojitos and desserts. It is made for families, friend groups and anyone chasing real flavor.
